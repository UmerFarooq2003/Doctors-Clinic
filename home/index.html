Genetic Counseling

Genetic counseling offers expert guidance to individuals and families, helping them understand and adapt to the genetic implications of diseases, facilitating informed decisions and risk assessment.

Who Needs It

  • Couples planning pregnancy, assessing risks for hereditary disorders.
  • Families with affected children planning future pregnancies.
  • First-time parents-to-be understanding child's disease susceptibility.
  • Late-life pregnancy considerations for genetic risks.
  • Genetic risks evaluation for cousin/caste marriages.

    • What does a Counsellor do?

  • Offers disease information
  • Uses pedigrees for transmission clarity
  • Estimates offspring risks
  • Explains disease process and discusses genetic testing
  • Suggests referrals and procedures

    • Our Services

  • Pediatric Genetics (childhood disorders), Oncogenetics (cancer genetics), Reproductive Genetics, Neurogenetics
  • Cystic Fibrosis, Down Syndrome, Thalassemia, Haemophilia, Cancers
